Our Dedicated Staff

Meet the experienced professionals committed to empowering youth through structure, education, emotional support, and positive reinforcement.

Leadership Team

Ed Young

Executive Director

Ed has led Heartland Boys Home since the beginning, bringing decades of experience in youth services and group home care. His vision and leadership, alongside Mark Billingsley, have shaped Heartland Boys Home into a place where young men working through trauma, chemical dependency, and behavioral issues can find stability, support, and hope for their futures.

Expertise: Program Leadership, Youth Development, Organizational Management

Mark A. Billingsley

Co-Founder & Leadership Team

Mark has been part of the leadership team alongside Ed since the beginning of Heartland Boys Home. Together, they strive for the best care possible for the boys, ensuring that every aspect of the program meets the highest standards of excellence and compassionate care.

Expertise: Program Leadership, Quality Assurance, Strategic Planning

Ryan Schroetlin

Program Director

Ryan is a former YRTC Geneva staff member with deep expertise in the juvenile justice system. His hands-on experience with system-involved youth brings valuable insight to our programming and helps ensure smooth transitions for our residents.

Professional Services

Licensed Therapy Services

We contract with licensed therapists who specialize in working with adolescents and trauma-informed care. Our therapeutic team provides:

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Group therapy and psychoeducation
  • Family therapy and involvement
  • Crisis intervention and support
  • Substance use counseling
  • Trauma processing and healing

24/7 Youth Care Workers

Our facility maintains around-the-clock supervision with trained youth care workers who provide:

  • Awake overnight supervision
  • Daily living skills support
  • Behavioral intervention and de-escalation
  • Recreational activities and engagement
  • Transportation and community outings
  • Crisis response and safety management

Our Commitment to Excellence

Training & Certification

All staff members receive comprehensive training in:

  • Trauma-informed care principles
  • De-escalation and crisis intervention
  • Child protection and safety protocols
  • Behavioral management techniques
  • First Aid and CPR certification
  • Mandatory reporting requirements

Background & Screening

Every staff member undergoes thorough screening including:

  • Comprehensive background checks
  • Child Abuse Registry clearance
  • Professional reference verification
  • Drug and alcohol screening
  • Ongoing supervision and evaluation
  • Continuing education requirements

Our Approach

Our staff team is united by a shared commitment to:

  • Treating every youth with dignity and respect
  • Building positive, supportive relationships
  • Maintaining high professional standards
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Collaborative teamwork and communication
  • Family-centered practice
