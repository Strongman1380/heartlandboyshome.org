A trauma-informed Group Home A in Geneva, Nebraska, housing male youth ages 12-18 working through trauma, chemical dependency, behavioral issues, and other challenges.

Our Mission

At Heartland Boys Home, our mission is to stabilize youth, support families, and build bridges to independence. We focus on helping young men understand their worth, learn life skills, manage emotions, and prepare for successful transitions back to home, school, and community.

What We Offer

Group Home Services

Our 16-bed licensed Group Home A operates 24/7 with daily structure, supervision, and personalized treatment planning.

Education

Fully accredited onsite classroom with certified special education teachers and individualized learning plans.

Mental Health Support

Licensed therapy including CBT, trauma processing, and substance use psychoeducation.

Our Philosophy

Every young person deserves dignity, safety, and a second chance.

Change happens through relationships, consistency, and support.

Healing requires addressing trauma, not just behavior.

Youth thrive where expectations are clear and accountability is balanced with compassion.
