Staff Directory

Meet our dedicated team of professionals committed to empowering youth through structure, education, emotional support, and positive reinforcement.

Leadership Team

Ed Young

Executive Director

Ed has led Heartland Boys Home since the beginning, bringing decades of experience in youth services and group home care. His vision and leadership, alongside Mark Billingsley, have shaped Heartland Boys Home into a place where young men working through trauma, chemical dependency, and behavioral issues can find stability, support, and hope for their futures.

Expertise: Program Leadership, Youth Development, Organizational Management

Mark A. Billingsley

Co-Founder & Leadership Team

Mark has been part of the leadership team alongside Ed since the beginning of Heartland Boys Home. Together, they strive for the best care possible for the boys, ensuring that every aspect of the program meets the highest standards of excellence and compassionate care.

Expertise: Program Leadership, Quality Assurance, Strategic Planning

Ryan Schroetlin

Program Director

Ryan is a former YRTC Geneva staff member with deep expertise in the juvenile justice system. His hands-on experience with system-involved youth brings valuable insight to our programming and helps ensure smooth transitions for our residents.

Expertise: Juvenile Justice, Program Development, Case Management
